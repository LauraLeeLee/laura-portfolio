Vernazza

Vernazza and the Cinque Terre. Cinque Terre means "Five Lands" and is made of up 5 fishing towns along the southern part of the Italian Riviera. There are trails that connect the towns. A few higher ones above the towns. Or one that snakes its way above the coast. The views are spectacular, the towns very quaint, though very packed with tourists- Italian and foreign. Vernazza was my favorite town. There was a different charm to it, a different 'feel' for me.
